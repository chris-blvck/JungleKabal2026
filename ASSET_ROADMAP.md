## NOTES FOR ARTIST

- All monster sprites: transparent PNG background, centered on canvas
- All icons: transparent PNG, works on dark (zinc-900) background
- Style: stylized tropical horror / jungle occult. Reference: Caribbean voodoo meets jungle adventure
- Color palette reference: deep greens, bone yellows, blood reds, abyssal purples for higher zones
- Elite variants: same base sprite but with a glowing color outline or tint shift (can be done in CSS/shader too)
- Rarity tint for weapons/artifacts: common=gray border, rare=blue border, epic=violet border, legendary=gold border (UI handles, icon just needs clean art)
- Monster portraits (80×80): cropped face/head shot of the mob sprite, used in combat enemy card
